Things To Know When Visiting Other Countries

  1. How to Greet People
    • While in Spanish speaking countries, say, "Hola, cómo estás?" This means, "Hi, how are you doing?"
    • While in Brazil, say, "Olá, como você está?" This also means, "Hello, how are you doing?"
  2. How to Ask for Help
    • While in Spanish speaking countries, say, "Necesito ayuda." This means, "I need help."
    • While in Brazil, say, "Preciso de ajuda." This also means, "I need help."
  3. How to End a Conversation
    • While in Spanish speaking countries, say, "Fue un gusto conocerte, adios." This means, "It was nice meeting you, goodbye."
    • While in Brazil, say, "Muito prazer, tchau." This means, "Pleasure meeting you, goodbye."
